2012 Season:

Game: Rebound Rumble

The 2012 game is designed similarly to basketball. Each alliance starts with 3 robots, and the robots have to shoot basketballs into hoops. However, there are obstacles to overcome when getting across the field. In the middle of the court, there are 3 bridges, which must be brought down in order to go over it, and between the bridges there is a 6 inch tall wall. The robot must be designed to traverse the bridge or go over the wall. Click on the image below for more information about Rebound Rumble!

     Why Should My Company Sponsor Team 3501?

Team 3501 is a non-profit organization, so we rely on the support of individuals and organizations in the community. Your support will help foster the next generation of:

  • Scientists
  • Engineers
  • Programmers
  • Businessmen/Businesswomen
  • Graphic artists

The different levels of sponsorship are:

  • Level 1: Nuts and Bolts; $100+
  • Level 2: electronics board; $500+
  • Level 3: Chassis; $1000+
  • Level 4: Robot; $2000+
  • Level 5: Honorary Firebird; $3501+
